Use the standard GitHub code of conduct.

## Our Commitment
We are committed to providing a respectful and inclusive environment for everyone.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful
- Be constructive
- Be collaborative
- Provide helpful feedback

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment
- Discrimination
- Personal attacks
- Spam

## Enforcement
Project maintainers have the right to remove contributions or restrict access if rules are violated.
